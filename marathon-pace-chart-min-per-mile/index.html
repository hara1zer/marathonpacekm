Min/mile FAQ

Is min/mile or min/km more accurate?

They’re the same pace expressed in different units. Use whichever matches your watch and your race signage.

Why do my mile splits look uneven on a km-paced plan?

Because 1 mile isn’t an even number of kilometres. Use average effort and lap splits rather than chasing perfection.

Should I use a pace band in miles?

If your race markers are miles (common in the US), a mile-based band can make pacing simpler. Otherwise km works well.
