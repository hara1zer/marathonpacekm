About

MarathonPaceKM is a small, lightweight site that helps runners convert a goal finish time into pace per kilometre, key checkpoints, and a printable pace band.

What you’ll find here

  • A homepage marathon pace chart (KM) + calculator.
  • Goal-time pages (e.g., 4:05, 4:50, 5:05) with splits, conversions, and pacing tips.
  • A monthly plan tool to help structure training (basic guidance, not coaching).

If you spot an error or want a goal time added, use the contact page.
